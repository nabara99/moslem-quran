# Privacy Policy for Moslem Quran

**Last updated: February 2026**

## Introduction

Moslem Quran ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- We collect your device's location data **only** to calculate accurate prayer times based on your geographic position.
- Location data is processed locally on your device and is **not** transmitted to our servers.
- Location data is cached locally to improve app performance and reduce battery usage.

### Notification Data
- We use local notifications to remind you of prayer times.
- Notification preferences are stored locally on your device.
- We do **not** collect or transmit any notification-related data.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:
- To calculate and display accurate Islamic prayer times for your location
- To send local notifications for prayer time reminders
- To improve app functionality and user experience

## Data Storage

- All data (location cache, prayer times, notification settings) is stored **locally on your device**.
- We do **not** have access to your personal data.
- We do **not** transmit any personal data to external servers.

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party APIs:
- **Aladhan API** (aladhan.com): To fetch prayer times based on coordinates. Only geographic coordinates are sent; no personal identifiable information is transmitted.
- **AlQuran Cloud API** (alquran.cloud): To fetch Quran text and translations. No personal data is transmitted.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security settings.

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed to be safe for users of all ages.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data (stored locally on your device)
- Delete your data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app
- Disable location permissions through your device settings
- Disable notifications through your device settings
